# 📢 Social Media Strategy

## 1. LinkedIn & Community

**Post Structure:**
1. **Hook**: The first sentence must grab attention (often controversial or question-based).
2. **Body**: Expand the topic with "breathing room" (blank lines between paragraphs).
3. **Takeaways**: Concrete value for the reader.
4. **CTA**: Discussion question or call to action.

**Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant ones tailored to your industry / niche.

## 2. YouTube (Video Descriptions)

- The first 2 lines are critical for SEO.
- Include links to related content / pages in the app or site.
- Use timestamps (chapters) if you have a transcript.
- Reference: `docs/resources/youtube-channels.md`.

## 3. Emoji

Use emoji sparingly, as bullet markers or emotional accents, not as word substitutes.
